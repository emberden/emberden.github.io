ore rebalance

about

This mod changes ore generation to make ore pockets much less common, but also much larger. Combine with mining rebalance to have fun constructing large mines and other infrastructure. Also removes dirt pockets from the lower strata of the world.

compatibility

Anything that edits ChunkProviderGenerate (yf.class) is incompatible.
